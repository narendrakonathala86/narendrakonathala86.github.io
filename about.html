About me

Hello, I’m Narendra Konathala, a full stack developer from Bangalore, India. I have 11.5 years of experience working with Infosys Limited, developing professional software for multiple engineering clients. My key specialization is in developing rich user interfaces for web, desktop and mobile applications.

I love creating intuitive and responsive user experiences, which I believe is a vital part in modern application development. Most of my development experience has been on creating lean architecture, consisting of light weight UI and data exchange formats to provide seamless experience to end users.

My other areas of expertise include maintaining high scalability and availability of the deployed products, design modular applications with loosely coupled architecture, train junior developers, perform product verification and validation, monitor database performance trends to identify and correct problems, diagnose usability issues, refine designs and performing release/configuration management using CI/CD principles.
