Structure Repair Manual Application

Project involves validation of Skin and other structural component data for various Airplane models to perform structural repairs. The tool is developed for Commercial Airplane Services team to provide aftermarket support to Airline customers in the event of structural repair of the airplane with an user-frienly interface.


Note : In order to maintain confidentiality, client name has not been disclosed. UI and data are mocked samples from actual implementation.

Composite fuselage skin module

One of the Airplane model has its fuselage completely built with composites. Unlike metallic parts , composites are made of different ply’s, hence the thickness of plies vary at different locations of the fuselage. The objective of this module is to fetch the skin thickness from composites and to visually represent at various locations of the skin.

My role is to gather requirements from various commercial airplane support team, create prototypes, gather usability requirements from end users, coordinate with offshore teams for implementation, application design and testing.

Section based visualization

This data originally was available in the form of paper-based manual or an electronic pdf file, and it was difficult for the user to locate information among the large data available. The tool helps the user to easy navigate to the desired effectivity, section, and sub section. With the options available in the application, user can visualize the right data with minimal manual intervention.

My role in the product is to utilize productivity improvement tools with automated tests and deployment process, SVG extraction, processing LWG from CGM and other CAD formats and proof of concept development for AR for smart repair. Additionally, I was also resposible for creating conceptual (business objects), logical (systems), and/or physical (database) models using latest software tools.
